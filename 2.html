MID-DAY MEALS

ZPHS Zuluru Schools Mid Day Meals Scheme will be launched in OCT 2007. There is also a separate room for cooking.Three ladies prepare food every day. The three ladies have been preparing food since the start of the Mid Day Meals scheme. The current schools have 346 students. Daily prepares food for 300 students.Teachers also have lunch at the school.The food is very tasty. The government pays for all the goods and materials needed for cooking. Every day there is a time table, according to which they cook. Every Wednesday and friday egg is laid out for each student. Special food items are cooked at events time like biryani rice, chicken etc.,
