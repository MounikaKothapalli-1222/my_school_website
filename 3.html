EVENTS

Many events are held at ZPHS Julur school,like Self Government Day, Children's Day, Teachers' Day, Farewell Party etc., and many more competitions.Science fair events are also held. Every year 2 students from 10th and 9th class will be selected and projects will be prepared and sent to the Science Fair compition. Government will give Money to prepare the project. In ZPHS julur Aug 15 and Jan 26 are very well celebrated. Big Help related events are also celebrated. (There are many details on the next page about Big Help. Please check it once) Students are very interesting and exciting at event time. They dance and sing at big events. Teachers teach dance to students. Aug 15 and jan 26 are celebrated very richly. Students dance on patriotic songs and sing good patriotic songs and Speeches are given.Julur Sarpanch and other elders attend all the events which are held at school. On Children's Day Teachers distribute chocolates to all students.Competitions are held for every Children's Day. Prizes will be given to those who win compitions like drawing competition, debate competitions, quiz games, essay writing, etc., On Teachers Days Students wish all the teachers, giving them gifts and honoring them. On Self Government Day some students should select the subject of their choice and teach that subject like a teacher for a whole day. Students are given prizes for best performance. 9th class students will give a party to 10th class students. Whole Schools will participate in these events. All 10th class students give speeches and share their memories. when teachers are transfor or retire the whole school will honor them and gives gifts to them and wish them.
